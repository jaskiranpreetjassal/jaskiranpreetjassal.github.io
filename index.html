Projects

Nexora AI Business Assistant

Nexora AI is my final-year project: a proof-of-concept business assistant chatbot designed to help users schedule, reschedule, cancel, and view meetings through text and voice input.

  • Built using Python, Flask, SQLAlchemy, SQLite, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
  • Implemented rule-based intent detection and slot-filling for missing meeting details.
  • Added meeting conflict checking with alternative time suggestions.
  • Integrated voice input using the Web Speech API.
  • Included authentication, password hashing, CSRF protection, rate limiting, and access-controlled routes.
  • Stored messages, meeting records, and event logs to support traceability and future process analysis.
  • Evaluated the prototype with users and documented strengths, limitations, and future improvements.
